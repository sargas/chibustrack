Welcome to Chicago Bus Tracker!

This Mozilla Firefox extension fetches Estimated Bus Stop Arrival Times and Service Route Bullitens from the Chicago Transit Authority's Bus Tracker API.

To begin seting up your routes, right-click on the CBT icon () at the lower left-hand side of the status bar.

Bus Stop Arrival Times

Track your most-used Bus Routes!

  • Right-click the icon,
  • click "Options"
  • click the "Add Stop" button
  • check the checkbox next to each of the stop numbers you'd like to watch

Now, just left-clicking the icon () will load up the latest predictions for the next arrival time.

Getting Service Bulletins

Service Bullitens, such as reroutes, changes to service times, etc., will appear in your status bar when the CTA announces them.

  • Right-click the icon,
  • click "Options"
  • click the "Add Route" button to add routes to Chicago Bus Tracker
