Chicago Bus Tracker

Hello, welcome to Chicago Bus Tracker. To begin configuring, right-click the little icon () at the lower left-hand side of the status bar.

Getting Service Bulletins

Right-click the icon, and click "Options". At the top is a button called "Add Route" which you may use to add routes to monitor. Notices will appear in your status bar when the CTA announces them (such as during reroutes, changes to service times, etc).

Bus Stop Arrival Times

Right-click the icon, and click "Options". Click the "Add Stop" button to choose the stop(s) you'd like to watch, and feel free to add as many stops as you wish. Now, just left-clicking the icon () will load up the latest predictions for the next arrival time.
